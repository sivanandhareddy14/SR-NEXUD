Every submission is automatically compared against: all other students' assignments, this student's own earlier submissions (previous semester / previous academic year), and an offline library of commonly-reused web phrasing. Marks & Grades CIE + SEE · auto SGPA · CGPA

Every subject's score is CIE (Continuous Internal Evaluation — Test 1, Test 2, Assignment, Quiz, Attendance, Practical/Lab, Project and any other components you define below) plus SEE (Semester End Exam). CIE marks are entered here by faculty/admin. SEE marks are uploaded, verified, and published exclusively by the Examination Cell / Admin (see the "Examination Cell (SEE Results)" module) — they appear below automatically, read-only, only after they have been published, and are locked from further edits until the Examination Cell unlocks them. Both CIE and SEE are automatically scaled down to their configured maximums, and the Total, average, SGPA, CGPA and credits are all calculated automatically. If raw CIE is below the eligibility minimum, the student isn't eligible to write SEE and the subject is a fail; otherwise scaled SEE below the pass minimum is also a fail. Students can view their own results here (read-only), and SEE marks will show as "Not Published" until the Examination Cell releases them.
